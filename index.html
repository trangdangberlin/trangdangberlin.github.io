HTML Cheatsheet

<h1> to <h6> tags, are used to provide titles for sections of content. <p>, <span>, and <div> tags specify text or blocks. The <em> and <strong> tags are used to emphasize text. Line breaks are created with the <br> tag. Ordered lists (<ol>) are numbered, and unordered lists (<ul>) are bulleted. Images (<img>) and videos (<video>) can be added by linking to an existing source.
